# Things to add in the future
## Data collection and analysis
* **Ore classification.** After each ore collection, check the white text on blue background at the bottom of the screen. There are 4 options: gold, bronze, silver or red background meaning that somebody had already mined the ore before you.
* **Storing data.** Using a .csv file store the statistics about the time of mining, quantity and kinds of ores. This might be done for further analysis and enhancing the bot.
## Bot effectivity changes
* **Down swipe.** Sometimes the bot can get stuck at a certain area of the mining territory. The solution would be to sometimes swipe the camera down so the bot aims at other ores and gets out of the endless circle.
* **Elimination of notifications.** After 4 hours of activity, a notification about a reward pops up. The solution: once a few miinutes tap on the place on the screen to get rid of it so the bot continues it's job.
